Our Menu

Dosa

A dosa is a rice pancake, originating from South India, made from a fermented batter. It is somewhat similar to a crepe in appearance.Traditionally, dosas are served hot along with sambar and chutney

Kaju Katli

Kaju katli also known as kaju barfi, is an Indian dessert similar to a barfi. Kaju means cashew; Barfi is often, but not always, made by thickening milk with sugar and other ingredients (such as dry fruits and mild spices)

Matar Paneer

Mattar paneer is a vegetarian North Indian dish consisting of peas and paneer in a tomato based sauce,spiced with garam masala.It is often served with rice and an Indian type of bread.
